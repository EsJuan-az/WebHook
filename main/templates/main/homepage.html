What is WebHook?

WebHook is a page that let you know and see different kind of sites on the web, it is a tool that is able to recieve recomendations of pages from its users and suggest them to other users

How to use WebHook?

Just pick an option, Get Hooked for going randomly or Categories for choosing the categorie of the sites that you want to visit

What do I have to do?

All you have to do in this case is allow us to open tabs in your browser, for this you can push this button, it will open a blank tab and you have to set the permissions active Open Tab
